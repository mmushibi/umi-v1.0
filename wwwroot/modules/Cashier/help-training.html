Point of Sale

Process sales transactions efficiently with the Point of Sale interface.

Main Functions:

  • Search and add products to cart
  • Apply discounts and promotions
  • Process multiple payment methods
  • Generate receipts and invoices

Step-by-Step Process:

  1. Search for products using barcode or name
  2. Verify product details and pricing
  3. Add items to the shopping cart
  4. Select customer (if applicable)
  5. Apply discounts or insurance
  6. Choose payment method
  7. Complete transaction and print receipt

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I process a refund?

Navigate to the Daybook, find the transaction, and click the refund button. Follow the prompts to process the refund.

What if a product is not found?

Check the spelling, try the generic name, or browse by category. Contact your supervisor if the product needs to be added to inventory.

How do I handle insurance claims?

Select the patient, verify insurance coverage, and process the claim according to the insurer's requirements.

What should I do if the system is slow?

Try refreshing the page, check your internet connection, or contact technical support if the issue persists.
