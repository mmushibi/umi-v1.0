Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new medication to inventory?

Navigate to Inventory â†’ Add Product. Fill in all required fields including Zambia REG Number, License Number, batch information, and expiry dates. Ensure all regulatory information is accurate before saving.

Category: Inventory Management

What is required for Zambia regulatory compliance?

All medications must have a valid Zambia Regulatory Number (ZAMBIA REG), proper license numbers, batch tracking, expiry date monitoring, and supplier information. Regular audits are performed to ensure compliance.

Category: Compliance

How do I handle prescription refills?

Go to Prescriptions â†’ Search patient â†’ View prescription history â†’ Select prescription for refill â†’ Verify refill eligibility â†’ Process refill with updated quantities and dates. Always check for drug interactions before refilling.

Category: Prescriptions

What should I do if a medication is expired?

Immediately mark the medication as expired in the system, remove it from available inventory, document the disposal process according to Zambia Pharmaceutical Authority guidelines, and notify your supervisor. Follow proper waste disposal procedures.

Category: Inventory Management

How do I check for drug interactions?

The system automatically checks for drug interactions when adding medications to prescriptions. Review the interaction alerts, assess severity levels, and consult clinical guidelines. Document any decisions to override alerts with appropriate justification.

Category: Clinical

How do I export inventory reports?

Navigate to Inventory â†’ Export CSV. Choose your preferred date range and data fields. The system generates a comprehensive report including stock levels, expiry dates, and regulatory information for compliance reporting.

Category: Reports
